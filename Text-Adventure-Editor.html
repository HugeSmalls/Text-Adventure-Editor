Make Your Own Adventure!

This is your game, and you get to write the story! Its like a choose-your-own-adventure book. Heres how to change it:

  1. Change the Start: Click in the "Starting Story" box and type the beginning of your adventure.
  2. Edit Story Pages: Click in the table boxes to change the story. Each row is a part of your game:
    • Page Number: A number for each part (like 1, 2, 3). Make each one different.
    • Story Text: Whats happening in this part.
    • Choice 1, 2, 3: What the player can do (e.g., "Open the door"). Leave blank if you dont need all three.
    • Next Page 1, 2, 3: The number of the page each choice goes to.
    • Item Change 1, 2, 3: Items to add (e.g., "key") or remove (e.g., "-key"). Leave blank if no items.
  3. Add More Pages: Click "Add New Page" to make a new row in the table. Fill it with your story details.
  4. Test It: Click "Play Game" to try your game. You can go back to edit anytime.
  5. Save Your Game: Click "Download Game" to save your changes as a new file. Open that file later to keep playing!

Tip: Look at the example rows to get ideas. You can make a space adventure, a haunted house, or anything you want!
